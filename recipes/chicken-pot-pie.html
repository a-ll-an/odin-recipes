Chicken Pot Pie Recipe

Description

This is a chicken pot pie recipe made with celery, peas, and carrots in a pre made crust. Every home cook needs one of these in their repertoire

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • vegetables
  • Butter & flour
  • seasonings
  • broth and milk
  • pie crusts

Steps

  1. Make the filling with the chicken and vegetables, broth and milk
  2. fill the chicken-vegetable mixture into the pie crust and bake
