Beans & Rice Recipe

Description

This is a basic vegetarian recipes that's filling and easy to make at home

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Onion and garlic
  • Rice
  • Broth
  • Beans
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook the onion and garlic until the onion has softened
  2. Stir in the rice then add the broth and bring to a boil
  3. Add the beans and spices
